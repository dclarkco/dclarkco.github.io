Use Latex formatting:

The formula for Euclidian distance is written as such in LaTeX:


						\[
						d(p,q)^2 = (q_1 - p_1)^2 + (q_2 - p_2)^2
						\]

Result:

The final product comes out looking like this:

\[ d(p,q)^2 = (q_1 - p_1)^2 + (q_2 - p_2)^2 \]
